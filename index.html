Biography

My name is Yaolei Shen (Chinese Charaters: 沈垚磊), pronounced as "Yao(垚) lei(磊)", "Shen(沈)". You can call me "Yao" for short.

Currently I am working toward the Ph.D. degree in robotics with Robotics and Mechatronics group at University of Twente, supervised by Prof. Antonio Franchi and Prof. Chiara Gabellieri.

Before I went to Twente, I obtained both my bachelor and master degrees in mechanical engineering from Department of mechanical engineering of Northwestern Polytechnical University, China, supervised by Prof. Wenjie Ge.
