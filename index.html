Research Interest

 I work in the field of micro aerial vehicle, bio-inspired robot, robot control. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Locomotion strategy of micro aerial vehicle
  • Nonlinear robust attitude control of flapping-wing aerial vehicle
  • Physical implements of bio-inspired aerial vehicle
  • Simpling-based motion/trajectory plan of mobile robot

Projects
  • 2019.09-NOW      Modeling and control for a bird-scale flapping-wing aerial vehicle (Master's project)[PDF]
    This project is relevant to my master's diploma project. In this project, the mathematic model of the previous flapping-wing aerial vehicle prototype was derived. Based on this model, the robust attitude controller and path tracking controller were also designed. And, these controllers will be implemented on the physical vehicle protype to test the performance.
